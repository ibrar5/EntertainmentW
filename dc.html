Best DC live action movies

Best DC live action movies

MCU stands for Marvel Cinematic Universe. It is currently world's greatest and most successful cinematic universe. It is based on Marvel commics. 25 movies and 4 TV series have been released since 2008. These movies are completely watchable for all age. If your age is more than 10, you can watch these movies. These movies are the best movies for teenagers in the world. Usually, 2 or 3 movies of MCU releases per year. So, we can say this universe is extending day by day. If you are a marvel commics lover, then what are you waiting for. You should watch this movies as soon as you can. There is not a single MCU movie which is not good, some are maybe not as good as expacted but some are mind blowing. So, let's see which movies have won everyone's mind and which have not.

    Doctor Strange is avery distinct movie like Guardians of The Galaxy. In this movie we have seen no known face. It is also a very good movie directed by Scott Derrickson.   • 6.Wonder Woman (2017)-Simon Cellan Jones  

    The First Season of Jessica Jones is unarguably one of the most important series of Netflix Marvel. In this show, very very important characters like Jessica Jones, Luke Cage have introduced. This show is the best Netflix Marvel show after the Daredevils and Punisher Season 1. Character development of this show is literally fantastic. The rivalry between Jessica Jones and Kilgrave was also amazing. Jessica Jones is a detective who is physically stronger than normal people. But, her idealism is not like a superhero at all, neither is she. She is a careless woman who don't think about herself. But, cares about people around her. But, she is not the main reason why this series is a really good one. The antagonist of this show, Kilgrave have made this show a very good one. He is basically a psychotic serial killer who have abilities, who deserves nothing but death. He does not care about anyone, even his own parents. He can controll people's mind. David Tennant really did a great job while casting this character. Honestly, I like the Kilgrave character more than Jessica. Because, I was able to hate him. But, on the other while watching Jessica Jones I really did not feel anything special. As a protagonist, her character development was not good at all. This is the only reason why this show is still a "Very Good" show.

  • 5.Justice League Snyder Cut (2021)-Zack Snyder  

    Daredevil is the first Netflix Marvel show. It is the show in which The Devil of The Hell's Kitchen rises. This show is a psychological show more than a superhero show. Because, the main hero Matt Murdock aka Daredevil is not a person with amazing superpower. On the other hand, the main anti-hero Wilson Fisk does not have any superpowers at all. Money and his ideology is what makes him a very powerful person. The main thing that made Daredevil a great show is it's protagonist and antagonist. It's not that Matt Murdock and Wilson Fisk have any personal relation. But their relation was more than personal just like Batman and Joker. Matt Murdock is a blind man who is a lawyer in the day and a masked and vigilante in the night who wants to protect his city. On the other hand, Wilson Fisk is a rich person who does not have any crime record. But, he had everything under his controll even FBI and ARMY. But, he is an anti-hero who cannot be hated. Because, his goal is right, and he cares very much about his lover, his best friend, his mother and his city even more than his life. He have killed a few characters in the show, but none of them were for his own happiness. He is a person who wants to rebuild his city, he is evem ready to sacrifice his life for this. Not only Netflix Marvel, Wilson Fisk is one of the best anti-hero in Marvel. On the other hand, Daredevil is one of the most loved Marvel Superhero. The saga between two person who have same goal but different way have made this series one of the best Marvel series.

From them, the main character was always a hero (Iron Man, Captain America, Thor etc.) or a team of heroes (The Avengers, Guardians of The Galaxy). But, Loki is different. Beacuse, Loki has always been the best anti-hero in MCU. Although, in this series none of the characters were very good or very bad. Loki went more deeper and deeper. He was discovering more and more unknown, surprising and shocking things. These surprising and shocking things are what which have made Loki a great series. I think, Loki is the funniest movie or series in MCU after Thor: Ragnarok. It is also the most misterious series in MCU. I mean you will not even know what will happen in the next minute. I don't know why but Loki was two step ahead from previous 2 MCU series Wandavision and Falcon and The Winter Soldier. I am saying it because Loki's first episode's veiwer was more than the previous series. I have already said, Loki is a series with a completely different and new concept. It's not that MCU never focused on the story of time. But, I have to say this none of those were focused like Loki's. Another important thing about this series is variant. Because, if you watch movie just for entertainment. If you always want to see something funny, action, then definitely this movie is not for you. If you watch movie to see what's happening around you in the society, then maybe Joker is for you. But, don't be careless while watching Joker, because just not your eyes, you even have to use your brain to while watchink this movie.
