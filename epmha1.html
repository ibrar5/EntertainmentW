My Hero Academia(2016)

Sub Dub

Midoriya Izuku was just a regular powerless middle school student in a world where 80% people have superpowers known as Quirks. However, he dreams of becoming a hero who can save everyone with smile like All Might, the world's gratest hero. After being the only one to try and save his childhood friend, Katsuki, from a Villain, All Might bestows upon him his own quirk, One For All. The story follows Izuku's entrance into U.A High School, a school that cultivates the next generation of Heroes.
